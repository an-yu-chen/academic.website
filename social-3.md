      placed me within a global academic environment where ideas, people, and
      networks expand and flourish.
    </p>

      I played a role in greeting and registering participants at reception, as
      well as supporting logistical coordination and participant communication.
      I was involved in ensuring that sessions ran smoothly, assisting
      participants, and responding to attendees’ questions regarding session
      rooms, schedules, arrangements, and other event facilities throughout the
      programme.
    </p>

    <p>
      It had a profound effect on me to witness how international researchers
      engage in dialogue. It also became clear that academic exchange is not
      only about presenting findings, but about creating space for shared
      understanding.
    </p>

    <p class="quote">
      Academic communities are built not only by ideas, but by the people who
      make those conversations possible.
    </p>

      I was fortunate to work with an amazing team during these conferences.

      academic communities are built collectively.
    </p>

    <p>
      We need to drive change, even through small organisational roles, because
      these roles support the larger structure of knowledge exchange. There are
      still many issues we need to address, from accessibility to inclusivity.

    <p>
